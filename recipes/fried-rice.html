Fried Rice

Home

Description

Fried Rice is a very delicious dish and quite easy to make. First of all you need to take some rice and wash it thoroughly from clean water, then soak them in the clean water for about 30 minutes. Alongside you can cut some vegetables of your choice. (Recommended) cut onions, tomatoes, potatoes and can take some peas too. After 30 minutes, take a cooker and heat some oil in it, add some cumin seeds in it after the cumin seeds gets a red-brownish colour, Add the onions to it, cook them till they get a yellowish texture or golden kind of colour on them, then add some tomatoes to it. Cook them for some time and then add other vegetables to it. Next step is to add Rice after removing the water from them in which they were soaked. After adding the rice, stir the mixture thoroughly. Next step is to add the spices. Take some salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder and add it to the mixture(their quantity depends on the rice quantity and varies differently). mix the mixture thoroughly after adding the spices. Add water to soak the mixture in it. Then Close the cooker lid. After a few whistles(3-5 typically) and some time of cooking, the Fried Rice will be ready to serve. Tip: Try it with Butter or tomato sauce.
