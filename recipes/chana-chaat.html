Chana Chaat (Chichpea Salad)

Description

Chana Chaat is a quite delicious and easy to make dish. Boiling some chickpeas and adding some raw vegetables and spices to it pretty much does the thing.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • chilli or chilli powder
  • salt
  • lemons

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas for 7-8 hours in clean water after washing them thoroughly
  2. After 7-8 hours Rinse them with clean water
  3. Boil them in the pressure cooker by adding water and salt to them
  4. when they are properly cooked and boiled, Take them out in a large bowl
  5. Cut a few onions and tomatoes in small pieces and add them to the bowl of chickpeas
  6. add some finely chopped chilli or chilli powder to the bowl containing other items
  7. now extract some lemon juice and it in it
  8. some more salt can be added according to taste
  9. stir it and mix it thoroughly and it is ready to serve.
